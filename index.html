About the programme

BSE and e-Careers have developed an exclusive programme, which provides you with the knowledge required and an easy to navigate social trading platform.

This programme educates everyday investors to navigate the financial markets, by providing education and mentoring form our seasoned experts. This is achieved through a combination of online training, as well as the oppertunity to meet our experts and benefit form their invaluable knowledge and experience

The programme consists of five distinct elements:

Exclusive
Access Event

Where you will have the opportunity to learn from and network with people who navigate the markets daily. You can learn behind the scenes tips and techniques and you can also share your strategies and bounce your ideas from seasoned professionals and fellow newbies who are on the programme. You will also be able to bring a guest.
